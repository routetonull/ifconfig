CCIE Hints

04 Sep 2016, 16:34
ccie / tutor

My company is looking for new hires to grow the Network Engineers team. As the only CCIE of the group I’ll be their tutor and do my best to help them to obtain the very same certification.

I’ve already worked as trainer for internal and external classes, up to CCNP level, but this is a different challenge.

The plan is to start from almost zero knowledge of networking up to CCIE in a time window of a little less than one year in the best case, two years at most.

I’ll try to share here my best advices for them but I think they’re valid for everybody starting the CCIE journey.

Plan you work, work your plan

Stress

Motivation

Learn to learn

Read a lot

Focus

Pareto

Join a community

Take structured notes

Onenote/Evernote

Think out of the box

Different solution for the same problem

Do not learn commands, lear protocols

When in doubt read the RFC

After fail don’t give up

Small % pass on first attept, use it to improve.

Speed

CCIE is not about typing speed. Less typing than expected.

Method

Config/Break/Fix

Read and understand command output / debug

…and in the end of that CLI commands!
