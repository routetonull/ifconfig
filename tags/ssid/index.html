inSSIDer

02 May 2012, 00:00
scanner / ssid / wireless

inSSIDer is a free tool to monitor and troubleshoot WiFi networks. Even if it isn’t as complete as other solutions like Chanalyzer or AirMagnet it can be used as a first step to check the channels usage, power levels and the mac addresses of the APs. Download it for free on the MetaGeek.com website.
