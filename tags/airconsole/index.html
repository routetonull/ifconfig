Airconsole

New toy: Airconsole

Monday, June 24, 2013
airconsole / getconsole / review / serial

Console access to devices for a network engineer is almost as important as telnet/ssh access. Usually an usb to serial adapter is required since almost all laptops don’t have a serial port. I must admit that a dual or quad serial adapter is in my Amazon wishlist ;-) I looked for wireless serial adapters, both bluetooth and wifi, and I even thought to build one myself with RaspberryPi. My final choice was Airconsole, now it’s time for a review.
